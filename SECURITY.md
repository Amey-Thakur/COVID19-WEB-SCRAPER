## Vulnerability Reporting
We prioritize the security and integrity of all archived engineering projects. If you identify a critical security vulnerability that could impact the broader collection or its users, please follow these reporting procedures:

1.  **Direct Communication**: Contact the Project Curator via the **[GitHub Profile](https://github.com/Amey-Thakur)**.
2.  **Required Information**:
    *   A technical description of the vulnerability.
    *   The specific component or logic block affected.
    *   Steps to reproduce or a Proof-of-Concept (PoC).
3.  **Timeline**: You will receive an acknowledgment of your report within 48-72 hours.
